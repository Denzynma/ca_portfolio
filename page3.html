Hank web page Case Study
(Focus on UI, UX and UX Writing)

This case study documents the process of redesigning Hank, a product by Patricia.

Company Overview

Patricia is a technology-driven alternative payments platform where merchants and consumers can transact with new digital currencies like bitcoin, gift cards and perfect money. HQ is in Lagos. Nigeria.

About Hank

Hank, a product by Patricia helps you track any missing or lost item by simply using a blue-tooth enabled device to track while attaching Hank on the item you want to track.

 Objective

 To redesign Hank web page in a way to increase user engagement and improve sales upon launch. Focus being basically on UX writing.

My Role

Product Design
Design Research
UX Writing

& design.

Hank current experience

Here is the current webpage view of Hank

The logo scrolls and overlap with the contents upon scrolling. There is no How it works on page or information.
The copy that says "No more missing keys, phones and wallets," limits hanks value. The copy, “With Hank you can do everything” does not set the context rather would leave users thinking differently. 

This screen here is not clickable. I think Information stating what the different Hank can do would help direct users and increase conversion. This would confuse users on what the different Hank does.

Here's the new result with iterations

I went with a realistic image that resonates with human's experience. Then, I changed the copy in a way it explicitly captures what hank does and it's values.

I added context to the different types of Hank so visitors of this page could see at a glance what is peculiar with each hank.

I introduced the easy ways Hank works on this page to further drive clarity.

 I also also put out the amazing features. Also, I changed the FAQ questions in a way it aligns with the copy. Firstly by explaining what Hank and other questions that could arise.

I added another CTA button instead of allowing the users to scroll back up they could take any action.
I added email field where users who aren't convinced or ready to pre-order could be notified once Hank is out. That way, the company could keep track of activities on the page.

I maintained consistency here with the countries. The current experience placed different countries with state in their category.

 Learnings

This project helped me realize how important it is to invest in your copy-writing so as to improve user engagement on your product.
