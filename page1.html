About Netflix

A streaming service that entertains members with movies and so on. In popular culture,
Netflix is often described as a companion to the lonely. 

The Challenge

Netflix is looking to partner with a local company to boost signups on their mobile web platform.
What company could best solve this problem? 

Competitive Analysis

I started with research. Getting to know how they solved similar product problem helped me  as a designer
define what creative  solutions could be implemented and improved to benefit both users and business.

Affinity Mapping

I personally mapped user flows of different use cases and it helped me identify and define problems, pain-points, and improvements that could be redesigned to enhance user experience in reaching their end goal.

  1. Data is expensive.
  2. Card error/rejection.
  3. Availability of Netflix originals on pirate websites.
  4. No authentication of account.
  5. They are aware of Netflix prior to the interview.
  6. Thinks the sign up flow is lengthy.
  7. Majority went with the basic plan.
  8. Thinks they can watch Netflix on mobile web
  9. Majority of the participants goes to pirate sites to download Netflix originals.

Based on user flow, I had proposed several solutions to improve it's current user experience by:

  1. Reducing the number of steps  required to signup.
  2. Redesigning functionalities that are more visible and discoverable.
  3.  Enhancing user feedback by disabling and enabling certain features and functionalities
  4. Incorporating a local brand to improve signup.

Final partnership choice

I reviewed the partnership choice. With critical research and brainstorming, I decided to go with
Barter, a product by Flutterwave.

About Barter

Barter, a product by Flutterwave is an online payment gateway that provides virtual dollar cards and you can pay for any subscription on any website with ease.

Why Barter?

  • A platform that excites young people
  • Widely used by people aged 18-45
  • Stress-free payment method
  • Eases stress of carrying the physical card by using their virtual card.
  • It has built a strong trust system for it’s users

How does this benefit Netflix?

Barter is offering 50% discount to stream Netflix for 6 months.

  1. This would project massive sign up as people would readily and easily create Netflix account.
  2. It would reduce patronage for pirate websites especially when Netflix releases trending movies to the that that can take a while to get to pirate websites. 
  3. Increase retention of subscribers after 6 months.
  4. Reduction of card rejection issues.
  5. Increase in brand awareness.

Usability testing feedback

Through the several stages of user testing, I had learned a lot about the product’s weakness, holes, strengths, improvements, & features that are needed to help grow and mature the product over time. The users loved the WhatsApp input to help them reach out to support when they have any challenge on the mobile web. They preferred the redesigned sign up flow.

Some of the central frictions that prevented users from reaching their goal were:

Iterations

Now that I refined the design direction, I continued my design process by prototyping. I realized that some
of the features were inconvenient and unclear. For example, the partnership discount and the swipe button
should be definite and clear for easy access. Moreover, I decided to add card dictator to help identify whether
it is verve, MasterCard, Barter card or visa.

I added more information on the movie preference screen to let users know that they can only preview
on the mobile web and watch movies only on the mobile app. So they don't feel being misled.

Solutions

The initial design as highlighted earlier lacked many important functionalities and improper use of UX
design that negatively affected many users' experiences. With the redesigned, I came up with solutions
based on research analysis and basic design principles to create a proper layout structure with hierarchy,
usability, accessibility in mind. Incorporating major requested features and core functionalities such as WhatsApp
functionality to make users contact support for an immediate response & other features to increase positive user experiences.

  1.  The initial extension does not have account authentication which I added
  2. I reduced the long sign up
  3. I made card rejection information more clearer 
  4. Went with the second variation

Empowering customization

One of the main objectives for this project was to increase sign up and to increase the trajectory of retention
for the business based on partnership choice. With the redesigned, I’ve resolved several significant problems by
improving the visibility of buttons and functionalities and partnership input with a discount that attracts users to
own account to help strengthen and enhance the product’s usability. I also incorporated multiple different features
to the signup flow based on user feedback, which was: clarifying through design that users can only preview movies
on the web and can only watch movies on Netflix app using phone, tablet and iPad, hide-show password, profile avatar,
and a majority of other product-related features to create delightful experiences in the product.
